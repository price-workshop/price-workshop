About the Workshop

The PRICE Workshop brings together researchers and practitioners from graphics, systems, networking, and HCI to address the unique technical challenges of competitive esports.

Esports is a major cultural and economic force, with competitions requiring cutting-edge technology for optimal performance. This workshop focuses on the intersection of hardware capabilities, software optimizations, and human perception that make competitive gaming possible.
